How it works

Three steps, zero configuration

01

Enable live captions

Turn on live captions in your Teams meeting with ⇧⌘A.

02

Start monitoring

Click 🔕 in your menu bar and hit Start Monitoring.

03

Get notified

A desktop notification fires the moment your name appears in captions.

Features

🔒

100% local

Reads the Teams UI directly. No audio, no network, nothing leaves your Mac.

Native Swift
